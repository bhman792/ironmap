The big idea

Know before
you go.

Every other app tracks what you did. IronMap also tells you what you're walking into. Members contribute real equipment data for their gym — machines, brands, conditions, and when something breaks.

01

Find your gym

Search by name, city, or zip. If your gym isn't there yet, add it in 30 seconds.

02

Confirm the equipment

Add machines from the global library. Set condition and weight range. You're the source of truth for your gym.

03

Log smarter workouts

Exercise suggestions come from what's actually on your gym's floor. History is tied to the specific machine — not just the movement.

04

Report issues in real time

Cable snapped? Weight stack stuck? Flag it. The next member sees it before they make the drive. Flags auto-clear after 30 days.
